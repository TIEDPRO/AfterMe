Founders & operators

Continuity isn’t only
for the cap table

You already have lawyers, accountants, and maybe a shareholders’ agreement. After Me fills a different gap: the moment when personal documents and instructions must reach the right people without your phone, your passwords, or your cloud logins.

We are the timing / delivery layer — an encrypted vault on your device, plus a Family Kit (QR card + .afterme file) so someone you trust can open what you prepared. See how access works in detail.

Why this shows up on a founder’s radar

Boards and insurers ask about business continuity. At home, the same logic applies in a different form: insurance policies, ID documents, instructions for your executor, and “where everything lives” notes.

Single place, your device

Store scans and notes in an encrypted vault that stays local-first. You choose what to add; categories help you organise documents in the app (identity, insurance, legal, and so on). When you create a Family Kit, it is one encrypted snapshot of your whole vault at that moment — every document stored in the vault then — not a separate export per category. Add or remove documents first, then generate a new kit if the handover should change. See how family access works.

Access without your credentials

The Family Kit is designed so your family (or your professional executor) does not need your Apple ID, two-factor device, or password manager. That is the point: independence from your accounts.

What After Me is not

Clarity keeps expectations aligned with regulators and your own advisors.

Not a will; not legal advice

After Me does not grant probate authority or replace a solicitor-drawn will. It holds documents and instructions you choose — alongside whatever professional estate planning you already have.

Not a B2B admin product

There is no separate “founder dashboard” or tenant console. This page explains positioning only; the app is the same consumer-grade vault and kit flow for everyone.

Long-term trust

The .afterme format is documented so your data is not locked to a single app forever. Premium includes a lifetime purchase option for those who want one payment and a long-term product commitment from us.

Prepare the layer next to your professional plan

Download After Me when it launches, add documents, and generate a Family Kit when you are ready. Share this page with a co-founder or partner if they ask how you are handling personal continuity.
