# After Me — Editorial calendar (SEO / blog cadence)

Lightweight plan aligned with **Phase 4** of `docs/core_additions_implementation_plan.md`: roughly **one long-form piece per week** for the initial quarter, with internal links between product pages and `web/blog/`.

## Conventions

- **UTM (optional):** `utm_source=site&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=blog&utm_content=<slug>`
- **Internal links:** Each new post should link to `/how-it-works`, `/`, and at least one related existing article.
- **Sitemap:** Add new URLs to `web/sitemap.xml` with `lastmod` when publishing.
